About Page

Take over Seattle in Risk City.

It's like the classic game of Risk except the board is dynamic and changing based on realtime data and events (well, it will be soon.)

Planned Features include:

  • - Dice roll battles to take over neighborhoods
  • - Multiplayer or computer opponents
  • - Real world conditions affect units (weather, traffic, neighborhood locations)
  • - Give your units a boost by checking in at real world locations
  • - Units with names!
  • - ...and more!

Risk City was built using Sails, Mongo, Angular and Materialize.
